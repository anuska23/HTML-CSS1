    About Royal Mountain Hotel

    Offering 4 on-site restaurants , 2 are seasonal and 2 are open year round, an outdoor pool and an indoor pool, Mountain View Grand Resort is located in Whitefield. The property features a spa and a tennis court. The accommodations at this resort will provide you with a private bathroom with bath or shower, TV and air conditioning. Some rooms have a mountain view. At Mountain View Grand Resort & Spa you will find meeting facilities, a game room and a kids' club. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking. The resort is 7.6 mi from Santa's Village, 23 mi from Cannon mountain and 21 mi from Mt. Washington Cog Railway.
